Our story

It all started as a competition...

In 2016 we (the founders of Instruqt) co-organised Hashiconf Europe. We came up with a great idea: let people solve challenges around the products of Hashicorp, and measure the time they required. The fastest player is the winner. This turned out to be a great success, so we repeated the same concept for other technologies.

The launch of our beta

In March 2017 we launched the first beta of Instruqt on Google Cloud Next. We developed competitions for the Google Cloud Platform, Elastic, Hashicorp and more. At the peak moment we had more than 1000 players competing. We got a lot of feedback, and people liked the way we teach new technologies.

Our mission

Over the last few months, we organised several competitions. We learned that some people like the competition element, but most people just want to learn new technologies. So we've pivoted towards a learning platform with a solid SDK. You can still create competitions, but our main goal is becoming the best hands-on learning platform.
