We Challenge you to Learn.

instruqt is a learning platform where you can compete in competitions consisting of challenges that gain you experience in a fun and hands-on way.

By moving to a gamified way of learning, you can learn more efficiently and simulate real life scenarios repeatedly.

  • Compete with others

    Compete with others to see how you stack up in a wide range of DevOps subjects.

  • Become an expert

    Level up your skills and experience by playing through scenarios of increasing difficulty from beginner, intermediate and advanced to expert.

  • Track your progress

    Get insight in your progress by seeing how far you, where your blind spots are, and how you compare to others.

  • Share challenges

    Contribute to the community by sharing challenges and solutions with others.

Plans

Basic challenges are always free. You can freely create and share these challenges with others.

Challenges that require infrastructure to be completed are paid to cover these costs. Pick and choose the challenges you want to participate in.

Create an organisation to get private leaderboards, full reporting and statistics.
